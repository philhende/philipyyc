Philip Henderson

Philip Henderson is a freelance artist working out of his beautiful hometown of Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Inspired by Abstract Expressionism, many of his works are studies in lines, shapes, and colour theory. Painting is Philip's latest venture in a lifetime of creative outlets. Phil hand stretches each canvas onto handmade frames, taking each work from beginning to end, creating a truly unique and custom piece of art. His preferred medium consists of raw unprimed canvas, acrylic paint, plasters and glue, which creates a bold textural aesthetic.

Philip is a proud resident artist of THE KNOW GALLERYa in Calgary, Alberta.
